This information may be used for advertising, fraud prevention, analytics, and service improvement.

App content, selections, or in-app interactions may be processed locally on the device to provide the expected user experience.

4. Log Data

The App itself does not run its own dedicated analytics backend. However, third-party SDKs and platform providers may collect limited log or diagnostic data for app reliability, ad delivery, and service performance.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not sell personal data. Limited technical information may be shared only with third-party service providers that support app functionality, advertising, or distribution.

6. Data Security

The App does not store personal data on its own servers. Any data processed by third-party services is handled according to their own security practices.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Choices

You can manage ad personalization and related preferences from your device settings where supported.
