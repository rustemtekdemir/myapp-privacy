Privacy Policy

App Name: FinanceCalculatorCenter

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the mobile application FinanceCalculatorCenter ("App").

1. Information Collection and Use

The App does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

Financial calculations performed within the App are processed locally on your device.

2. Advertising

The App may display ads using third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect and process limited technical information, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information such as model, OS version, and language settings
  • Ad interaction and performance data
  • Diagnostic information
  • Approximate IP-based location information

This data may be processed for advertising, fraud prevention, service reliability, and performance improvement.

3. Calculation Results and Sharing

The App may allow users to share calculation results using the native sharing features of the device. Shared content is initiated only by the user.

Calculation results are generated locally and are not sent to the developer’s own servers.

4. Log Data

The App does not operate its own analytics servers. However, third-party SDKs and platform services may collect limited usage, diagnostic, or technical information for app stability, ad delivery, and fraud prevention.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not sell personal data. Limited technical information may be processed by third-party services used for advertising or platform functionality.

6. Data Security

The App does not store personal data on its own servers. When third-party services are involved, they handle data transmission and security according to their own policies and systems.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Choices

You can manage ad-related preferences from your Android device settings, including resetting or limiting your Advertising ID where available.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
