Privacy Policy

App Name: AirfryerCards

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the mobile application AirfryerCards ("App").

1. Information Collection and Use

AirfryerCards does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or precise location.

The App is designed to let users browse and use recipe content in a simple and practical way.

2. Advertising

The App may display advertisements using third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect and process limited technical information, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App interaction and ad performance data
  • Approximate IP-based location information

This data may be used for advertising, fraud prevention, analytics, and service improvement.

3. Recipe Content and App Usage

The App provides recipe and cooking-related content for informational purposes only. Users are responsible for checking ingredients, cooking times, temperatures, and food suitability according to their own needs.

4. Log Data

The App itself does not operate dedicated analytics servers. However, third-party SDKs and platform services may automatically collect limited diagnostic or usage-related data for reliability, advertising, and performance measurement.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not sell your personal data. Limited technical data may be processed by third-party service providers used by the App, such as advertising and platform services.

6. Data Security

The App does not store personal data on its own servers. When third-party services are used, data transmission is handled over secure connections where applicable.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Choices

You can manage advertising preferences from your device settings, including resetting or limiting your Advertising ID, depending on your Android version and settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
