Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use this mobile application ("App").

Information Collection and Use

The App itself does not require you to create an account and does not ask for personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

The App displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain information to provide and measure ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (a device identifier used for advertising)
  • Approximate device information (such as device type, OS version) and ad performance data

This data may be collected and shared with Google for advertising purposes. Google’s use of data is governed by Google’s own policies.

Log Data

The App does not run its own analytics servers. However, third-party services (such as AdMob/Google Play services) may generate limited diagnostic information for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and performance measurement.

Data Security

The App does not store personal information on its own servers. Data exchanged with third-party services (such as AdMob) is transmitted over secure connections (e.g., HTTPS) where applicable.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can control ad personalization from your device settings (for example, by resetting your Advertising ID or adjusting ad personalization settings on Android).

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
